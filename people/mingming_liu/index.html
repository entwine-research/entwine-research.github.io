ABOUT MINGMING

Dr Mingming Liu is currently working as an Assistant Professor in the School of Electronic Engineering at Dublin City University (DCU). He received his B. Eng (1st Hons, ranked 1st) in Electronic Engineering from the National University of Ireland Maynooth in 2011 and his Ph.D. from the Hamilton Institute at the same university in 2015. He has several years of experience in machine learning, control and optimisation theories with strong links to electric vehicles, IoT, smart grids, smart transportation and smart cities.
