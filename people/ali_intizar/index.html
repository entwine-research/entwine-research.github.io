ABOUT ALI

Dr. Ali Intizar is Assistant Professor in the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Dublin City University. His research interests include Internet of Things (IoT), Real-time Data Analytics, Machine Learning and AI for IoT, Edge Computingt and Semantic Knowledge Graphs. He is actively involved in various nationally and internationally funded research projects aimed at providing IoT-enabled adaptive intelligence for various domains including smart cities, smart buildings, smart farming and Industry 4.0. Dr. Ali Intizar obtained his Ph.D. (with distinction) from Vienna University of Technology, Austria in 2011.
